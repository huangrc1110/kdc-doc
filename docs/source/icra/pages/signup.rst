Signing Up
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The sign-up process is simple and intuitive. Follow these easy steps for the competition sign-up:

- Open our competition's official website using this link provided `here <https://www.kdc.icra.lejurobot.com/home>`_
- Click the "Join Now" button as shown on the main page, and simply fill in your personal information as prompted to sign up for this competition

    - Note: For individual signups, school/company name can be left blank, Any teams in the signup information are not to be recorded as competition teams. For teaming up for the competition, please do so after everyone's signup have been completed

Logging In
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- Open our competition's official website using this link provided `here <https://www.kdc.icra.lejurobot.com/home>`_
- Locate the profile icon on the top right corner of the website, click on that, and log in using your credentials as from the previous section.
